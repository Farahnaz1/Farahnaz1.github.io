What is Ikebana

Ikebana is a Japnese art of floral design. In ikebana flowers, stems, and leaves are arranged in specific styles to create a unique and attrative arrangement. Furthermore, special tools and vases are used to create these arrangments. In Japan there are many schools to teach this art. Also, here in Canada chapters provides the oppertunities to learn this unique art. Ikebana has very strict and defined rules to create the visually appealing floral arrangements. In Japan there are more than 3000 schools of ikebana. The basic tools required for ikebana are very simple. The things you need are a pair of scissors or shears for cutting the materials and a kenzan to hold your materials. With these tools you can create your ikebana arrangement. Ikebana is famous all over the world. In Ottawa, many ikebana instructors are available to provide training to learn this art. Many online courses are also available as well.
